Preliminary policy

The preliminary policy is intended to shape some basic rules which aid in creating a respectful and relaxed channel atmosphere.

  • Be respectful to others
  • Take into consideration other people's concerns regarding persistent bias or non-understanding
  • Since the channel is intended for discussion, it would be helpful if people didn't post links exclusively, without having ideas or opinions on the subject
